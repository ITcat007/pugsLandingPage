Health Guarantees

Breeders will provide a written health guarantee for their puppies because they are confident in the genetics behind their puppies. There’s no reason for them to hind anything.

Health Clearances

While breeders might not complete some health clearances on very young puppies, reputable breeders will have complete health clearance information for both parent dogs.

No Sale Sight Unseen

No reputable breeder would have sold their puppies sight unseen. Because they care for their dogs, it’s important for a breeder to see buyers before selling dogs to them and for you to see a puppy before buying one.

Vaccinations

Any responsible breeder will make sure that their puppies are up to date on their vaccinations before sending them home to you.The breeders will provide you a proof for this, and the vaccinations must follow the Official Standarts

Age Appropriate Sale

The first 8 to 12 weeks of a puppy’s life are crucial to their health and socialization, and the mother dog plays an essential role in this. For this reason, reputable dog breeders will not separate puppies from their mothers until a minimum of 8 weeks old.

Willingness to Answer Questions

Any responsible breeder will be willing to answer any questions you might have about their breeding program, dogs, and distinctions.They often encourage questions and will have plenty of their own.
